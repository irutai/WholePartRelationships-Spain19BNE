Exploration of Whole–Part Relationships in the "Biblioteca Digital Hispánica"

This website presents an online navigation tool that facilitates the analysis of the structure of authors' works for both the general public and researchers. Through this tool, we aim to show how the different editions of a given author’s works in the Biblioteca Nacional de España are related to each other.

These relationships have been identified automatically using an innovative methodology that directly analyzes the text extracted from digital documents through OCR technology, eliminating the need for additional processing.

For each of the authors considered, you can view their relationship graph by clicking on their name on this page. A new page will open showing the works included and their relationships. Hovering over a work will display a small window containing links to both the Biblioteca Nacional de España catalog and the digitized work. On the right side of the graph window, you will find several options to adjust the presentation of the data.

The set of relationships displayed focuses on the Spanish19BNE collection, which comprises approximately 12,000 volumes in Spanish written by 559 different authors who were born and died in the 19th century or the early decades of the 20th century, each with at least 10 distinct works.
